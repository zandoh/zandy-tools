Dept. of Modularity

The Workshop Doctrine

Detachable by design

Each module is a separate LoadOnDemand addon. Switch one off and it is never loaded — zero memory, zero CPU, zero residue in the gearbox.

Featherweight chassis

No minimap button, no broker feed, no stray frames cluttering your interface. Just the tools, mounted on a lean Ace3 frame.

One control console

Every module is configured from a single settings panel, with preferences stored per character — your tank and your healer each keep their own calibration.

Patent Schematics

Schematic Nº 001 Approved

Role Check

“The Automatic Role-Acknowledger”

Answers LFG role checks and group role polls with your preferred roles the instant they appear. No more racing the timeout popup, no more queuing as the wrong spec.

  • Accepts LFG role checks with your configured roles
  • Answers group polls by Tank → Healer → DPS priority
  • Offers only roles your class can perform
  • Holds fire in combat to avoid protected-function errors
Schematic Nº 002 Approved

Gear Check

“The Deficiency Spotter, Mk. II”

Flags items on your character panel that are missing enchants, gems, or open sockets — before the Great Vault reminds you the hard way.

  • E / G / S indicators directly on equipment slots
  • Hover any flag for a plain-English explanation
  • Item-level threshold skips low-level gear
  • Each check toggles independently, updates live
Schematic Nº 003 Approved

Keystone Reminder

“The Keystone Recollection Bell”

Rings in with a popup after every Mythic+ completion showing exactly which keystone is now in your bags — dungeon and level — so you never push the wrong key.

  • Appears seconds after each M+ completion
  • Reads the real dungeon and key level from your bags
  • Dismiss with one click or Escape
  • A quick reminder, not another interface
Operating Instructions

One Lever Runs the Works

/zt

Type /zt (or /zandytools) to open the control console. Enable or disable each contraption, adjust its calibration, and carry on — settings are kept per character.
